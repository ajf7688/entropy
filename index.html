What's Next?

Entropy

Entropy studies emerging technologies which impact consumer behavior and related economic changes. With analysis of public data feeds our solutions can comprehend sentiments around an upstart innovation.

Community First

Entropy is a community-first initiative started in July 2017, the core team functions as a DAO or Decentralized autonomous organization, we also collaborate with self-employed Individuals/Groups who are part of our community.

"The future does not like being predicted, We like to (scientifically) predict the future" - Entropy

We ask the question, What's Next? Whats the next innovation? the next industry to experience disruption? the next emerging technology?
With our unique blend of combining various fields of study, along with data science + software engineering expertise, we attempt to find out.
