Our Solutions

Acirra is an on-chain analysis tool to study public blockchains like Bitcoin & Ethereum, used to derive insights from transactions in realtime.
Acirra was first conceptualized in 2016 and built as a side project with beta releases used internally.
In Jan 2018, a full v1 was released as an API for developers. We also started TEC (The Entropy Community).
Over the course of the next 2 years, Acirra enabled insights across 250+ metrics for users.
In 2020, the solution stack was acquired by a blockchain researcher.
